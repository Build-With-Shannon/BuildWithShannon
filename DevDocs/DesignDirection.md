Design a high-fidelity desktop homepage for a personal brand website called “Build With Shannon.”

This should feel like a premium personal brand headquarters for a modern builder, teacher, and creator focused on smart solutions with AI. The design should feel structured, elegant, polished, feminine, modern, and visually distinctive.

Important:
- desktop homepage only
- high-fidelity UI
- polished and intentional
- not a portfolio site
- not a SaaS landing page
- not a generic personal brand template
- use strong spacing, refined hierarchy, and modern editorial structure

Brand direction:
Build With Shannon is a personal brand focused on building, teaching, and creating smart solutions with AI. The homepage should communicate credibility, structure, clarity, and thoughtful design.

Use this color system:
PRIMARY PURPLE #6C3EF4
  - for primary buttons, CTAs, and major accent elements
Deep Charcoal #1F2430
Soft Gray #E9EAF0
OFF WHITE #F8F9FC
  - as the main page background
Muted Violet #B7A6F8
  - for secondary accents, tags, decorative lines, and icon details

Typography and feel:
- strong typography hierarchy
- elegant mix of bold, semi-bold, and thin text
- premium spacing
- subtle decorative line details
- clean section transitions
- structured but warm presentation

Create the homepage in this order:

1. Header
- logo text: Build With Shannon
- navigation: About, Learn, Systems, Work With Me, Resources, Contact
- right-aligned primary button: Explore

- left side includes:
  - small eyebrow text

- headline text:
  - Shannon Reed.
  - Building, Teaching & Creating

- text styling:
  - first line bold
  - second line semi-bold
  - third line thin

  - a large branded placeholder for a professional headshot or structured visual composition

Overall goal fo0 the hero section :
Create a bold, premium hero section with the same composition and energy as the reference image: dark purple cinematic background, strong left-aligned text block, and a professional portrait on the right. The result should feel polished, modern, confident, and high-end.

Layout:
- Use a two-column hero layout
- Left side = text content
- Right side = large portrait image
- Keep the text vertically centered relative to the image
- Maintain generous horizontal padding and strong spacing
- Make the hero feel wide, balanced, and clean

Background styling:
- Use a deep dark-purple gradient background
- Add subtle glowing horizontal light streaks across the middle/lower area
- Include soft ambient purple lighting and a slightly futuristic, editorial-tech feel
- Keep the background smooth, premium, and uncluttered
- No busy patterns
- No obvious UI cards or floating elements
- The background should support the portrait and text, not compete with them

Text block styling:
- Place the text on the left side
- Use a large, bold, highly legible headline
- Make the headline white with strong contrast
- Use a stacked multi-line headline layout
- Keep line spacing tight and clean
- Make the heading the dominant element on the left side
- Add two CTA buttons below the text
- Buttons should be aligned horizontally with a small gap
- Keep the left column compact and visually strong

Portrait styling:
- Place a large professional portrait on the right side
- Crop the portrait so it feels similar to the reference: upper body / seated professional composition
- The portrait should blend naturally into the background
- Keep the image sharp, polished, and premium
- Make the portrait feel like the anchor visual of the hero
- Avoid boxed image frames or card containers
- The portrait should feel embedded into the hero background, not placed on top of it

Visual tone:
- modern
- elegant
- structured
- premium
- confident
- feminine but strong
- editorial-tech
- polished personal brand

Buttons:
- Primary button should use a rich purple fill
- Secondary button should be darker and more subtle
- Both buttons should feel clean, modern, and compact
- Slight corner radius
- Strong contrast against the background

Important changes to make:
- Increase contrast and drama in the hero
- Make the layout feel more cinematic and premium
- Make the right-side portrait larger and more integrated
- Make the text block feel cleaner and stronger
- Reduce anything that feels flat, generic, or template-like
- Match the mood and composition of the reference image, not just the colors

Do not:
- do not add extra cards
- do not add icons in the hero
- do not clutter the background
- do not use a light theme
- do not make it look like SaaS UI
- do not make it feel like a portfolio header

The final hero should feel like a high-end personal brand homepage for a builder, teacher, and creator using AI.
